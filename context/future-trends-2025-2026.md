# Future Trends: 2025–2026 Signal Scan

## Social Trends: The Human Response to a "Decade of Disorientation"

**The Trust Recession:** UK consumer trust in the government fell to 43% by Nov 05, 2025. 72% of shoppers are now willing to switch to cheaper brands due to a "trust deficit" regarding fairness and transparency.

**Nouveau Nihilism & Individualism:** Disenchantment with global systems is leading people to "live for the now". This "Escape to Individualism" prioritizes personal growth and autonomy over traditional milestones like home ownership.

**Strategic Joy:** In 2027, consumers will use "Strategic Joy" as a tool to combat prolonged stress and emotional dysregulation, embracing play for connection and inspiration.

**The "Affection Deficit":** As AI automates interactions, there is a growing premium on "Analog Soul" and the "imperfectly human".

**Conservative Shift:** In Brazil, 49% of the population shows high levels of conservatism, with 60% wishing the country would "go back to how it was before".

## Technological Trends: From Hype to Agentic Reality

**Agentic AI Expansion:** 2026 marks the shift from AI assistants to "Agentic AI" — autonomous systems that execute end-to-end tasks and manage complex business workflows.

**AI-Native Development:** By 2030, 40% of enterprise applications will be built using AI-native platforms, enabling "tiny teams" to deliver software at unprecedented speeds.

**Quantum Readiness:** Forward-thinking organizations are already preparing for "Quantum Advantage," with leaders 3x more likely to belong to multiple collaborative ecosystems.

**Physical AI:** The integration of AI into robotics and drones is creating "Physical AI," driving new requirements for data centers and cooling infrastructure.

**Generative Engine Optimization (GEO):** As users move from search engines to "answer engines" (e.g., ChatGPT, Gemini), brands are shifting strategies to GEO to ensure they are cited in AI-generated answers.

## Economic Trends: Resilience Amidst "Weaponized Interdependence"

**K-Shaped Growth:** While global GDP growth is projected at 3.2% for 2025, the recovery is "K-shaped," with middle-to-low income consumers remaining pressured by everyday expenses.

**Trade Uncertainty:** The "One Big Beautiful Bill Act" (OBBBA) and U.S. tariffs are expected to initially heat up inflation in early 2026 before growth slows later in the year.

**Blue-Collar Renaissance:** AI is eroding entry-level white-collar roles in law and finance, leading Gen Z to pursue stable, high-paying "AI-proof" trades like HVAC, welding, and solar installation.

**Transactional World Order:** Global trade is moving toward "world ordering" — a state where order is created daily through negotiations and "brutally transactional" bilateral deals rather than lasting institutions.

## Environmental Trends: Adaptation and Resource Scarcity

**The Adaptation Advantage:** Consumers are moving from "eco-anxiety" to "tactical adaptation," settling into a rhythm of living with constant environmental and economic crises.

**Climate-AI Paradox:** While climate change remains Risk #1 for experts, it is increasingly "crowded out" of consumer minds by immediate concerns like inflation.

**Circular Economics:** Retailers are shifting toward "circular models" (reduce, reuse, recycle, resell) as a core business strategy rather than just a marketing claim.

**Renewable Acceleration:** Photovoltaic (PV) solar energy is becoming a "cornerstone" of the global energy transition, though supply chain reshoring is creating new regional cost dynamics.

## Political Trends: Fragmentation and Sovereignty

**Geopolitician-in-Chief:** The U.S. "Liberation Day" tariff campaign has accelerated the transition to a multipolar world, forcing a renegotiation of global trade rules.

**AI Sovereignty:** 93% of executives believe they must factor "AI Sovereignty" (local data residency and control) into their 2026 strategies to navigate geopolitical tensions.

**Democracy Under Strain:** Experts cite "Systemic Political Fear" (31%) and the "crisis of liberal democracy" as major emotional drivers for 2026.

**Weaponized Interdependence:** Global fragmentation is deepening as nations use trade and finance as instruments of political power.
