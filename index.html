The Opportunity

Three forces are reshaping
the future of hospitality.

01
AI Reshapes the Journey
AI is reshaping the customer journey end-to-end — from discovery and booking to in-stay experience and loyalty. Winning this moment requires more than strategy or technology alone. You'll need to win not only the infrastructure layer, but the experience layer designed on top of it. Choice Hotels' proprietary tech stack creates a rare structural advantage.
02
Experience Over Amenities
Across hospitality, the premium market has become increasingly competitive and increasingly easy to replicate. Finishes are more refined, menus more considered, yet experiences feel interchangeable. The next frontier of differentiation is not what a space contains or looks like — it's how it behaves, how it recognizes you, and how it makes you feel.
03
Loyalty is Being Redefined
Loyalty is often reduced to status tiers and transactional rewards — acknowledged, but not deeply felt. The most compelling growth opportunities for Choice Hotels may not come from within hospitality at all. New verticals and experience ecosystems can transform a hotel brand into a platform for the full arc of how people live, travel, and belong.
